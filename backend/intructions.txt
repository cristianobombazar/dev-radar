How to start?
  node index.js (without 'live reload')
  yarn nodemon index.js (with live reload)
  yarn dev (with live reload)

Glossary
  -D (Dev dependencies)


STEP 1
  yarn init -y

STEP 2
  yarn add express

STEP 3
  create index.js  

STEP 4
  yarn add nodemon -D

STEP 5
  yarn add mongoose;

STEP 6
  yarn add axios;
